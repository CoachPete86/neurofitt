Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the programme?
The programme includes the full training guide, interactive workbook, exercise demos and nutrition guidance. Higher tiers add live coaching and advanced tracking.
Do I need any special equipment?
No. The Starter tier is completely equipment‑free. Higher tiers offer optional equipment‑based progressions.
Can I upgrade later?
Yes. You can upgrade from Starter to Pro or Elite at any time by paying the difference. Your progress carries over.
Is this programme suitable for corporate teams?
Absolutely. We offer corporate packages and licences. See the corporate page for details.
What if I have ADHD?
NeuroFit was designed with neurodiverse individuals in mind. Sessions are short, varied and incorporate cognitive drills to improve focus and impulse control.
How is this different from a regular workout plan?
Traditional programmes focus solely on the body. NeuroFit integrates brain training, mindset work and lifestyle coaching for a holistic approach.
How long does the programme last?
You can complete the core programme in 6–8 weeks, but many users continue to use the workouts and metrics indefinitely.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. You have a 14‑day cooling‑off period from the date of purchase in line with UK consumer law. If you have not downloaded or consumed the digital content during this period, we will process a full refund. Once you start accessing the content, you waive your right to cancel (see our returns policy for details).

Guarantee & Refund Policy

We stand behind the quality of NeuroFit. If you decide the programme isn’t right for you within 14 days of purchase and you haven’t started accessing the digital content, simply email us at support@neurofit.co.uk for a full refund.
