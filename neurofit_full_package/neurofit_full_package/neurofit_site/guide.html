Quick Start

The Quick Start section gives you everything you need to begin your NeuroFit journey today. NeuroFit combines short, high‑intensity workouts with cognitive drills to improve focus, coordination and resilience. To get started:

  • Download the interactive workbook from your welcome email.
  • Watch the quick‑start video below for an overview of the programme.
  • Choose your track (equipment‑free or gym‑based) and plan your first week.
  • Commit to at least three sessions per week. Consistency beats intensity.

No‑Equipment Track

Designed for those without access to a gym, the no‑equipment track emphasises bodyweight movements and plyometrics. Each session balances cardio bursts with mobility and cognitive drills. Typical exercises include:

  • Dynamic Warm‑up: marching, arm circles and hip openers to prepare your joints.
  • Strength Circuit: push‑ups, air squats, lunges and plank variations.
  • Cognitive Drills: reaction time games, pattern recall or dual‑task exercises to sharpen focus.
  • Cool‑down: stretching and breathing exercises to lower your heart rate.

You can complete these sessions anywhere – in your living room, a hotel room or outdoors – making it easier to stay consistent even when travelling.

Exercise Library

The NeuroFit exercise library provides demonstrations and coaching cues for every movement in the programme. Each exercise includes a description, benefits and video demonstration. Highlights include:

  • Bear Crawl: builds shoulder stability and coordination.
  • Single‑Leg Deadlift: improves balance and posterior chain strength.
  • Box Breathing: calms the nervous system and trains concentration.
  • Memory Ladder: cognitive drill that challenges working memory under fatigue.

See the Exercise Demo Library Plan for a full list of exercises and filming guidelines.

Meal Planning

Nutrition supports your training and mental performance. Our approach is flexible and focuses on whole foods, balanced macros and hydration. Guidelines include:

  • Prioritise protein (1.2–1.6 g/kg bodyweight) to aid recovery.
  • Choose complex carbohydrates (oats, sweet potatoes, fruit) to fuel sessions.
  • Include healthy fats (nuts, seeds, oily fish) to support brain health.
  • Stay hydrated – aim for 2–3 litres of water per day.

Use the Macro Calculator in the workbook to tailor meal portions to your goals. For ADHD individuals, consistent meal timing and avoiding processed sugars can help stabilise energy and concentration.

Progress Tracking

Tracking your workouts, nutrition and subjective state keeps you accountable and highlights improvements. The interactive workbook includes:

  • Exercise Log: record sets, reps, weights and cognitive drill scores.
  • Food List: note meals, water intake and mood to identify patterns.
  • Weekly Planner: schedule sessions, rest days and life commitments.
  • Progress Dashboard: charts your consistency, strength gains and mental clarity over time.

Review your data weekly to celebrate wins and adjust your approach. Small, continuous improvements compound over months.

Advanced Metrics

For those who want deeper insights, NeuroFit supports advanced metrics such as heart rate variability (HRV), reaction times and cognitive performance scores. You can integrate wearable data (smartwatch or heart rate strap) with the workbook to track recovery and readiness. Over time you’ll see how sleep, stress and nutrition affect your training output.
