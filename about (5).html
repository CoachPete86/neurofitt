About NeuroFit

NeuroFit blends practical exercise, mindset training, and applied neuroscience to improve focus, resilience, and wellbeing.

Qualifications & Approach

  • Level 3 Personal Trainer, Strength & Conditioning CPD
  • Applied breath training and CO2 tolerance methods
  • Behaviour change frameworks and habit design
  • Accessibility‑first programming for home or gym

Philosophy

Consistency beats intensity. We design sessions that are easy to start and rewarding to complete. Data informs progress, not perfectionism.

LinkedIn • support@neurofit.example

Method in brief

  1. 5 sessions/week for 45–60 minutes
  2. Strength + aerobic base with embedded cognitive drills
  3. Nutrition targets by mass and goal
  4. Workbook‑based tracking for visibility and accountability
