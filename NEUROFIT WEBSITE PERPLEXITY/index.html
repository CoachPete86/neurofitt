Medical Disclaimer

Important Health Notice

The NeuroFit programme is for educational and fitness purposes only and is not intended as medical treatment or diagnosis.

Consult Your Healthcare Provider

Before starting any exercise programme, especially if you have:

  • ADHD or other mental health conditions
  • Physical injuries or limitations
  • Cardiovascular conditions
  • Any chronic health conditions
  • Are taking medications

Exercise Risks

All physical activity carries inherent risks including:

  • Muscle soreness or injury
  • Cardiovascular stress
  • Falls or accidents
  • Aggravation of existing conditions

ADHD Considerations

While exercise can benefit ADHD symptoms, it should not replace prescribed medications or professional treatment. Always work with your healthcare team.

Stop Exercise If

  • You feel pain or discomfort
  • You experience dizziness or nausea
  • You have breathing difficulties
  • You feel unwell

Professional Guidance

NeuroFit coaches are fitness professionals, not medical doctors. Our advice is based on exercise science and wellness principles, not medical diagnosis or treatment.

Assumption of Risk

By participating in NeuroFit programmes, you assume full responsibility for your health and safety. You understand the risks and participate voluntarily.

Emergency: If you experience serious symptoms during exercise, stop immediately and seek medical attention.
