NeuroFit — 12‑Week Plan (Minimal‑Kit)

Track: Minimal‑Kit. Sessions are 45–60 minutes. Keep pain-free, RPE ≤8. Warm-up and cool-down required.

Week 1 (Phase I — Foundation)

DaySessionFocusPrescription
MonPhase I — Foundation — Strength AMovement quality
  • Add light DB/KB if available: Goblet squat 10, DB row 10/side
  • Circuit x3: Squat-to-chair 12, Elevated push-up 8–10, Backpack row 12/side, Dead bug 10/side
  • Cognitive: 2 min Stroop at RPE 3–4
  • Finisher: Zone 2 walk 10 min
  • RPE ≤7
TuePhase I — Foundation — IntervalsAerobic power
  • 8×30 s brisk stair/step or march / 60 s easy
  • Box breathing 4×(4-4-4-4)
WedPhase I — Foundation — Strength BUnilateral control
  • Split squat 10/side, Hip bridge 12, Pike push-up 8, One‑arm row 10/side, Side plank 30 s/side ×3
  • Cognitive: 2 min tap‑reactions
ThuPhase I — Foundation — Mobility + WalkRecovery
  • Walk 30–40 min easy
  • Mobility flow 15 min
FriPhase I — Foundation — EMOM 20Mixed capacity
  • Min1: 12 squats • Min2: 8 push‑ups • Min3: 12 hip hinges • Min4: 30 s fast step‑ups (repeat)
  • Reaction drill 90 s
SatPhase I — Foundation — Active RecoveryLow load
  • Walk 45–60 min or yoga 20–30 min
SunPhase I — Foundation — Off / PlanPlanning
  • 10‑min review. Set 3 micro‑commitments.
