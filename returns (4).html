Returns & Refunds

Immediate digital delivery means the statutory 14‑day cancellation right may not apply once access is provided. Regardless, we honour a 30‑day performance guarantee: complete ≥8 sessions, keep dated logs, and if no progress in any tracked metric, email refunds@neurofit.example within 30 days for a full refund. Refunds are paid to the original method within 5–10 business days once eligibility is confirmed. This does not affect your statutory rights.
