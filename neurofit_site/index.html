Train your brain and body together

NeuroFit is a cognitive‑physical training programme that fits into your busy life. Boost focus, resilience and energy with science‑backed workouts designed for neurodiverse individuals and high‑performing teams.

Why NeuroFit?

Integrated Training

Every session blends physical exercise with cognitive drills to enhance neuroplasticity and mental agility.

Designed for Busy Lives

Workouts are 20–30 minutes long and can be done anywhere – no equipment required unless you choose to level up.

Evidence‑Based

Built on research linking movement, focus and memory, with guidance on nutrition and recovery.

Interactive Workbook

Our companion workbook (a Google Sheets template) lets you log workouts, plan meals, calculate macros and visualise progress. Duplicate it into your own account and start tracking today.
