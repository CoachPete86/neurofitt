Returns & Refunds Policy

This policy explains the circumstances in which you may cancel your purchase of NeuroFit products or request a refund. It applies in addition to your statutory rights under UK consumer law, including the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013.

Digital products and services

NeuroFit programmes are supplied as digital content and services. Under the Consumer Contracts Regulations, you have a legal right to change your mind and cancel within 14 days of purchase. However, this right is lost once you start downloading or streaming the digital content, provided we have obtained your consent and acknowledgment that the cancellation period will be lost by commencing the download【21252875622574†L81-L87】.

During checkout you will be asked to confirm that you agree to immediate performance of the contract and understand that you will lose your right to cancel once the download or streaming of the content has begun. By checking this box and completing the purchase, you consent to the digital delivery beginning immediately and acknowledge that your right to cancel is therefore waived【21252875622574†L144-L169】.

Money‑back guarantee

Although the statutory cooling‑off period does not apply after you access the digital content, we want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If, within 7 days of buying a NeuroFit programme, you are genuinely dissatisfied for any reason, please contact us at support@neurofit.co.uk to request a discretionary refund. Refunds will be considered on a case‑by‑case basis and may be refused if you have accessed a significant portion of the programme, downloaded resources or breached our terms of use. We cannot guarantee a full refund once content has been consumed.

Subscription cancellations

If your purchase is part of a recurring subscription, you may cancel future payments at any time by logging into your account or contacting support. Your access to the programme will remain active until the end of the current billing period. No partial refunds are provided for unused time.

Tangible goods

Should we supply any physical goods in future (e.g. branded merchandise), you will have a statutory right to cancel within 14 days of delivery. Goods must be returned unused and in their original packaging. You are responsible for the cost of return shipping unless the item is faulty or misdescribed.

How to request a refund

To request a refund, please email support@neurofit.co.uk with your order number, date of purchase and reason for the request. We aim to respond within 2 working days and will process any approved refund to the original payment method within 7 working days.
