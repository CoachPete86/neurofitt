Quick Start

  1. Pick your track: No‑Equipment or Minimal Kit.
  2. Schedule five sessions this week in your calendar.
  3. Complete the Baseline Test (12‑min walk/step test + attention drill, see below).
  4. Start Phase I, Week 1 Day 1. Keep RPE ≤ 7/10.

No‑Equipment Track

Use bodyweight, a backpack, stairs or a single step, and floor space. Follow the Foundation plan with EMOMs and circuits.

  • Warm‑up 5–8 min. Mobility each session.
  • Stop for sharp pain. Modify or regress movement.
  • Nasal breathing during low‑intensity work.

Exercise Library

See the Exercise Library plan for full cues and safety notes. Core list: squat, hinge, push, pull, lunge, carry, anti‑rotation.

Examples
  • Goblet Squat — knees track toes, brace, full foot contact.
  • Kettlebell Deadlift — hinge at hips, neutral spine, pack lats.
  • Push‑Up — straight line, elbows ~45°, full range.
  • Row — lead with elbow, pause at rib line.

Sample Week

Phase I example. See programme design for full 12‑week plan.

  • Mon: Strength A + Zone 2
  • Tue: 8×30 s intervals + box breathing
  • Wed: Strength B + 2‑min attention drill
  • Thu: Mobility + 30–40 min walk
  • Fri: EMOM 20 mixed
  • Sat: Active Recovery
  • Sun: Off + plan

Meal Planning

Targets per day: protein 1.6–2.2 g/kg; fats 0.6–1.0 g/kg; carbs scaled to training load. Hydration 30–40 ml/kg.

Example Day
  • Breakfast: eggs + oats + fruit
  • Lunch: chicken, rice, mixed veg
  • Snack: Greek yoghurt + berries
  • Dinner: salmon, potatoes, salad

Progress Tracking

Use the Interactive Workbook. Track sessions, RPE, steps, sleep, and attention drill scores. Review weekly trends.

Advanced Metrics

  • Zone distribution and time in zone
  • Work capacity per session (reps × load)
  • CO2 tolerance (breath‑hold protocol)
  • Recovery time to resting HR
