Frequently asked questions

Is this safe for beginners?

Yes. Start with the No‑Equipment track and keep RPE ≤7/10. Seek medical advice if unsure.

What equipment do I need?

None for Starter. Pro benefits from a pair of dumbbells or a kettlebell.

How long are sessions?

45–60 minutes, five days per week, with two recovery days.

Does it help ADHD?

It targets attention, working memory, and stress tolerance via combined physical and cognitive drills.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. Pay the difference within 30 days and we unlock higher‑tier assets.

Do I need a gym?

No. Minimal‑kit options are provided for home or gym.

How do refunds work?

Email your logs to refunds@neurofit.example within 30 days if no progress after ≥8 logged sessions.

What if I have an injury?

Use mobility and reduced range regressions. Stop for pain and seek clinical guidance.

Is payment secure?

Yes. Stripe processes all payments. We do not store card details.

Do you offer corporate pilots?

Yes. See the Corporate page or email corporate@neurofit.example.
