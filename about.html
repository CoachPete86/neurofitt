About the NeuroFit Creator

Hello, I’m Pete Ryan, a personal trainer, mindset coach and two‑time full‑contact British kickboxing champion. I’ve spent nearly two decades helping clients transform their bodies and minds through movement, mindset and resilience training. NeuroFit is the culmination of my experience as a professional athlete and coach working with neurodiverse individuals, busy professionals and corporate teams.

Qualifications & Achievements

  • Level 3 Certificate in Personal Training (UK), CPD in Exercise Referral and Nutrition.
  • Two‑time full‑contact British kickboxing champion.
  • Completed three 160–180‑mile ultramarathons across the Amazon, Namibia and Kenya.
  • Certified mindset coach with specialism in ADHD and executive function.

Insurance & Professional Standards

I maintain full professional liability and public liability insurance through a UK‑accredited insurer. All coaching practices follow UK industry standards and are updated regularly to reflect current research and guidelines. Safety and efficacy are always paramount.

Philosophy

I believe physical training is inseparable from mental performance. NeuroFit integrates strength, cardio, mobility and cognitive drills to build a more resilient you. The programme respects your busy life and unique challenges – whether that’s ADHD, corporate stress or simply finding time to move. By training your brain and body together, you’ll unlock greater clarity, focus and vitality.
