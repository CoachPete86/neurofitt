NeuroFit for Cognitive Performance at Work

Abstract: How integrated physical‑cognitive training improves focus, stress tolerance, and productivity, and how to pilot NeuroFit in a small team within 30 days.

The Problem

  • Rising cognitive load and sedentary behaviour reduce attention and output.
  • Standard wellness perks lack measurable impact.

The Science Signal

  • Exercise elevates neurotrophic factors; attention training improves task‑switch efficiency.
  • Combined protocols improve adherence and transfer.

The NeuroFit Method

  • Five sessions/week, 45–60 min, with 2–3 min cognitive drills.
  • No‑equipment and minimal‑kit tracks.

Pilot in 30 Days

  1. Week 0: Baseline metrics (attention, 12‑min step/walk test, stress survey).
  2. Weeks 1–3: Programme rollout with workbook tracking.
  3. Week 4: Post‑test and ROI snapshot.

KPIs & ROI

  • Attendance ≥ 70%
  • Attention +10–20%
  • MVPA +20–40%
  • Stress −10–20%

Implementation

  • Privacy‑first data collection; anonymised dashboards.
  • Accessibility and inclusion notes.
