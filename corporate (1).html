Corporate Wellness Programmes

NeuroFit helps businesses create healthier, more resilient teams. Our corporate wellness offering combines short, engaging workouts with cognitive training and mindset coaching. The programme is designed to fit into the working day and can be delivered on‑site or remotely.

Executive Summary

Stress and cognitive overload reduce productivity and increase absenteeism. NeuroFit targets these issues by training employees’ bodies and brains simultaneously. Benefits include improved focus, reduced stress, better teamwork and a stronger company culture. Programmes are scalable from small teams to enterprise‑wide rollouts.
