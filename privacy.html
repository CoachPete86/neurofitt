Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how NeuroFit collects, uses and protects your personal data when you use our website and services. We comply with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Data Controller

The data controller is Coach Pete Ryan (trading as NeuroFit). Our contact email is privacy@neurofit.co.uk.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data:

  • Identity & Contact Data: name, email address and billing address when you purchase a programme or sign up for our newsletter.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type and device information collected via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Usage Data: pages visited, interactions with our site and engagement with emails.
  • Health & Training Data: optional information you enter into the workbook regarding exercise, nutrition and wellbeing.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Provide and deliver the programme you purchase, including customer support.
  • Process payments via our payment processor.
  • Send you transactional emails and marketing communications (with your consent).
  • Understand how visitors use our site via analytics to improve our services.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process your data only when we have a lawful basis. The bases we rely on include the performance of a contract (providing the programme to you), our legitimate interests (to improve our services) and your consent (for marketing emails and analytics). You may withdraw your consent at any time.

5. Data Sharing & Transfers

We share your data with trusted third parties only where necessary, such as our payment processor (Stripe), email service provider and analytics provider. These providers act as processors and are contractually bound to protect your data. We do not sell or rent your personal data. If we transfer your data outside the UK, we implement appropriate safeguards in line with the UK GDPR【880451403464607†L125-L205】.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described above. Technical and analytics data are typically kept for 14 months. Invoice and financial records are retained for six years to meet tax obligations.

7. Your Rights

You have rights under the UK GDPR, including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to our processing of your personal data and the right to data portability【880451403464607†L216-L223】. You also have the right to withdraw consent and to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO)【880451403464607†L238-L251】. To exercise any of these rights, please email privacy@neurofit.co.uk.

8. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies to make our website work and, with your consent, analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our site. For details on the cookies we use and how to manage your preferences, see our Cookie Policy. We do not set non‑essential cookies unless you have accepted them【727433046591268†L409-L420】.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page and, where appropriate, notify you by email.
